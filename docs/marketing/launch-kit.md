# InfraForge Brand Hub Launch Kit

## Purpose

This launch kit packages `temitayocharles.online` as the public InfraForge brand hub. It is not a résumé launch. It is a product narrative launch for an AI-native infrastructure and governed AI systems ecosystem.

## Launch thesis

InfraForge builds governed AI systems and the infrastructure they need to operate reliably.

The public story should stay simple:

- AI models need model operations, not just demos.
- Infrastructure needs operational intelligence, not more scattered alerts.
- Personal AI needs useful delegation with user control.
- Mobile AI needs fast access without collapsing into a toy chatbot.
- Technical education needs practical product-building paths.
- Secure platforms need repeatable delivery, observability, secrets, and runbooks.

## Audience

### Primary

- AI infrastructure teams.
- Platform engineering leaders.

- Technical founders building AI-enabled products.
- Teams evaluating governed AI operations and model operations.

### Secondary

Temitayo Charles is the builder behind InfraForge: an AI-native platform and DevOps engineer focused on Kubernetes, GitOps, DevSecOps, model operations, operational intelligence, governed AI workflows, and productized technical education.

## Launch narrative pillars

### 1. Model operations

AI Inference Lab shows that AI models need serving boundaries, benchmarks, metrics, runtime packaging, and capacity planning.

### 2. Operational intelligence

ForgeWatch shows that infrastructure signals need context, triage, KeepHQ integration, Kubernetes state awareness, and operator authority.

### 3. Governed personal AI

Project Iris shows that personal AI should help with everyday digital work while keeping memory, approvals, tools, providers, and auditability under user control.

### 4. Mobile AI interaction

Jerry shows that AI interaction should be accessible from mobile surfaces for commands, status, approvals, and lightweight assistant workflows.

### 5. Secure platform foundation

InfraForge Platform and Vault Ops show the infrastructure discipline underneath the ecosystem: Kubernetes, GitOps, secrets, observability, runbooks, and reliable delivery.

### 6. Practical education

Young Coders and AI Builders Academy turn coding, AI, and platform knowledge into structured learning experiences.
