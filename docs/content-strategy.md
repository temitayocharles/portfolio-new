# Content Strategy

## Positioning

This portfolio should position Temitayo Charles Akinniranye as a serious engineering practitioner across:

- Cloud Engineering
- DevOps and automation
- Kubernetes and GitOps platforms
- Platform Engineering
- DevSecOps and secrets management
- Observability and reliability
- AI infrastructure and AI-assisted operations
- Network engineering and troubleshooting fundamentals
- FinOps-aware infrastructure decisions

This is not only a DevOps portfolio. It should show a blended engineering identity: infrastructure, automation, production operations, secure systems, and applied AI.

## Brand Promise

Temitayo designs, builds, secures, monitors, and documents production-style platforms. The work should communicate ownership and maturity without exaggeration.

Preferred language:

- Designed

- Deployed
- Automated
- Secured

- Integrated

## AI Presence Requirements

AI should appear throughout the portfolio in a credible way:

- AI infrastructure as a project category.
- AI-assisted operations as a case study theme.
- AI automation as part of platform workflows.
- AI builder or agent systems where actual project evidence exists.
- Responsible AI boundaries and operational guardrails.

Do not overclaim AI model development expertise unless verified by real implementation. Prefer precise phrasing such as:

```text
Built infrastructure and workflow patterns for AI-enabled operations.
```

instead of:

```text
Built advanced AI models.
```
