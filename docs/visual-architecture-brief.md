## Recommended Copy Blocks

### Architecture Strengths

- Well-structured multi-layer enterprise architecture.
- Strong use of Kubernetes, GitOps, and CI/CD automation.
- Observability-first operating model.
- Security and governance embedded across platform layers.
- AI-assisted operations without unsafe autonomous writes.
- Reusable workflows and production-style documentation.

### Product Value Proposition

- Accelerate delivery through automated CI/CD and GitOps.
- Reduce operational risk through observability and SRE practices.
- Improve reliability with runbooks, alerts, and feedback loops.
- Strengthen security with scanning, secrets, RBAC, and policy gates.
- Enable intelligent operations through AI-assisted evidence gathering.

### Recommended Improvements

- Add product-grade architecture diagrams to key repositories.
- Write case studies using problem, solution, impact, and stack.

## Design Rules

- Use clean enterprise styling.
- Prefer 8 to 10 layers maximum.
- Avoid excessive icons and tiny labels.
- Use plain terms that recruiters and engineering managers understand.
- Do not include credentials, customer identifiers, private URLs, or unapproved production details.
